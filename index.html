About Leah

Born and raised in Brooklyn, NY I always loved computers. I had my first computer at the age of 10 years old and fell in love with technology and design. I went to William H. Maxwell CTE High School to study business. To my surprise the curriculum changed from business to graphic design and that’s when I first learned graphic design. I fell in love with graphic design and decide to pursue it in college. I received a Bachelor’s in Technology at the New York City College of Technology in 2011. However while I was in HS I also had a passion for the web. In 2007 the web wasn’t anything like it is today and I wanted to study web development. My good friend told me about the Flatiron School. I applied for the Web Development Fellowship and got in. It was really exciting as well as really hard but I got through it and I landed an apprenticeship at Constructive in NYC. During my apprenticeship I learned a plethora of technologies and was exposed to the real world of helping clients with professional websites. I learned PHP, WordPress, Drupal, Gulp, Sass, Client Relations and QA. I recently was hired as a Full-time Web Developer. If you have any questions or just want to chat you can email me at hey@leahjs.me or leahj.suter@gmail.com. Thank you.
