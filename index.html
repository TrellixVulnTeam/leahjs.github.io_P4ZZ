About me

My name is Leah and I am a Frontend Web Developer located in Brooklyn, NY. I've worked for Constructive for about 6 years. We are a web design agency that work on WordPress websites primarily for social impact and higher education clients. Prior to Constructive I was a graphic designer.

I love web development and I want to continue pursuing it as a career but I did find a new love for digital marketing. I am currently taking classes and pursing certifications.
